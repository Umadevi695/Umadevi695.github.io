My Services

UI/UX Design

UI (User Interface) Design focuses on the look and feel of a product — colors, buttons, typography, and layouts. UX (User Experience) Design focuses on the overall experience — making sure the product is easy, smooth, and enjoyable to use.

Web Design

It involves building websites and web applications. It has two main parts: Frontend: What users see (HTML, CSS, JavaScript). Backend: Server, database, and application logic (languages like PHP, Python, Node.js).

App Design

It focuses on designing apps specifically for smartphones and tablets. It includes UI/UX principles optimized for smaller screens, touch interactions, and mobile performance.
